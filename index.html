About Me


Highlights of my work

These are some of my graphic design projects and illustrations that I created over the past year. My favorite piece is the Jazz Illustration, one of my most recent works. The bar depicted in the artwork is a real place located in Williamsburg, which inspired the atmosphere and details of the piece. I used watercolor and colored pencils to bring the scene to life, capturing the warm, vibrant energy of live jazz and the cozy mood of the bar.
